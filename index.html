Work Process

My approach ensures every website I build is aligned with your business goals. Here's how I turn your vision into reality — step by step.

Get Ideas I begin by understanding your business, audience, and goals.
Sketch Up I create wireframes or sample layouts to visualize your website’s structure.
Discuss I go over the initial designs together and fine-tune the direction.
Revise I implement changes based on your feedback to get everything just right.
Approve Once you’re fully satisfied with the site, you give final approval.
Launch I publish the site, ensuring it's fully functional and optimized.
